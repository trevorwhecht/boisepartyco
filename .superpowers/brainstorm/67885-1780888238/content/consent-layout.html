Compliance note

Under TCPA, service cannot be conditioned on agreeing to receive marketing texts. Since "create an account" is always a third path, users are never forced to opt into SMS/email — this is compliant. The SMS checkbox must never be pre-checked. The required disclosure text (Msg & data rates may apply · Reply STOP to cancel · Privacy Policy link) must appear beneath the SMS checkbox.
